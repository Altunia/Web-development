SEO

SEO involves optimizing your website so that search engines like Google can better understand and rank it.

SEO Types:

SEO can broadly classified into four types

On-page SEO, off-page SEO, technical SEO and local SEO

On Page SEO:

It refers to the process of optimizing individual web pages to improve their rankings
and attract more relevant traffic from search engines. It involves making changes to
the content, HTML source code, and overall structure of a webpage to make it more search-engine-friendly and user-friendly.

Off page SEO:

focuses on activities outside the website to improve its authority,
trustworthiness, and relevance. This includes building high-quality backlinks,
promoting content on social media, earning brand mentions, and engaging in
influenceror guest blogging collaborations. It signals to search engines that
the site is credible and worth ranking

Technical SEO:

Technical SEO involves optimizing the technical aspects of a website to ensure that search engines
can crawl, index, and render the site efficiently. It includes improving site speed, mobile-friendliness,
secure connections (HTTPS), structured data, and resolving issues like broken links or duplicate content.

Local SEO:

Local SEO is the process of optimizing a website to increase visibility in location-based searches. It
focuses on improving rankings for queries that include a specific geographic area, such as "near me"
searches or searches that involve cities, states, or neighborhoods. Key elements include optimizing
Google My Business, collecting local reviews, and ensuring NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency.
